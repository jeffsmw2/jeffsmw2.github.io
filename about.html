About Us

We are the IEEE Student Branch of UBC, representing IEEE, the world’s largest association in technology. 

As the largest IEEE student branch in North America we are actively involved in the events and activities dedicated to developing the professional skills of engineering students.

Our vision is to promote the academic excellence and professional development of Electrical and Computer Engineering students.

The UBC Electrical and Computer Engineering Student Society (ECESS) is assisting with the financing and organization of this event. The ECESS represents over 1000 undergraduate students and serves to promote the academic, professional and social well-being of students through a wide variety of events.
