Think Engineering 2017

10th Annual Networking Event

Think Engineering is an annual networking event organized by the IEEE Student Branches at UBC, SFU, and BCIT in conjunction with the UBC ECE Student Society. We strive to connect between industry and promising, enthusiastic students.

We are pleased to announce the tenth annual Think Engineering event, which will be held on February 3, 2017 at the Sheraton Wall Centre, from 5 PM to 10 PM.

Tickets are $30 for IEEE members and $35 for non members.

Tickets are limited and can be purchased at MacLeod 319, at the UBC campus. There will also be a networking workshop prior to the event; this will be held on January 27, 2017 from 6.30pm to 9pm in KAIS 2020/2030. The dress code for the event is business formal, which means no red jackets! Additionally, there is no need for resumes, but bringing business cards is recommended.
