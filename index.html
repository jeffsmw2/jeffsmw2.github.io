Think Engineering 2020

13th Annual Networking Event

Think Engineering is an annual networking event organized by the IEEE Student Branches at UBC, SFU, and BCIT in conjunction with the UBC ECE Student Society. We strive to connect between industry and promising, enthusiastic students.

We are pleased to announce the eleventh annual Think Engineering event, which will be held on January 31, 2020 at the Sheraton Wall Centre, from 5 PM to 10 PM.

Tickets for students are $20 for IEEE student members and $25 for student non-members. Tickets for industry representatives are $80.

Tickets are limited so get yours soon! Students and industry representatives can purchase their ticket on Eventbrite. If you are an IEEE student member, please select the IEEE Member Admission and enter your IEEE membership number to obtain the student membership discount. Students may also purchase tickets at MacLeod 319 located in the UBC Vancouver Campus. A networking workshop is available for students in preparation for the event and will happen on the evening of January 29, 2020 on the UBC Vancouver Campus, in Fred Kaiser Building room 2020/2030 from 5pm to 6pm. The dress code for the event is business formal. Additionally, there is no need for resumes, but bringing business cards is recommended. You can check out attending companies here.
