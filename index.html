Think Engineering 2016

9th Annual Networking Event

Think Engineering is an annual networking event organized by the IEEE Student Branches at UBC, SFU, and BCIT in conjunction with the UBC ECE Student Society. We strive to connect between industry and promising, enthusiastic students.

We are pleased to announce the ninth annual Think Engineering event, which will be held on January 29, 2016 at the Sheraton Wall Centre, from 5 PM to 10 PM.

Registration is now available in Macleod 319. Limited tickets, so get them ASAP.

Event Highlights

  • Cocktail Reception

  • Three course dinner

  • Keynote speech given by a Platinum sponsor industry leader

  • Open panel discussion with Platinum and Gold sponsor representatives

  • Open networking session

About Us
