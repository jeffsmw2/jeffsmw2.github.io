Think Engineering 2018

11th Annual Networking Event

Think Engineering is an annual networking event organized by the IEEE Student Branches at UBC, SFU, and BCIT in conjunction with the UBC ECE Student Society. We strive to connect between industry and promising, enthusiastic students.

We are pleased to announce the eleventh annual Think Engineering event, which will be held on January 26, 2018 at the Sheraton Wall Centre, from 5 PM to 10 PM.

Tickets prices for students will be announced shortly. Tickets for industry representatives are priced at $80.

Tickets are limited and can be purchased at MacLeod 319, at the UBC campus. Online payment will be available shortly. There will also be a networking workshop prior to the event. The date and time will be announced shortly. The dress code for the event is business formal, which means no red jackets! Additionally, there is no need for resumes, but bringing business cards is recommended. You can check out attending companies here.
